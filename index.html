1st & Pike

1st & Pike is our first location. We have lots of cookie flavors there and we hope you like them all. Cohoreos, Chinickerdoodles, Salmonimal Crackers, Chocolate Chip "Hookies", and Do-Si-Cohos -- we try to satisfy all palates.

SeaTac Airport

SeaTac Airport is your best choice when you fly. It's the perfect snack to take on the airplane. Hearty and satisfying when flying for business or pleasure -- plus no weird smells on the plane. Our new Flying Fish cookie gives you wings !

Seattle Center

After a hard day of poetry or roller derby at Seattle Center , a cookie or two is a great way to rest and refresh. A Chinookie or a King Mint satisfies your softer, more cultured side and if you're sweaty after a derby bout, we have a great shirt !

Capitol Hill

Capitol Hill BLOCK PARTY ! What goes better with eleventy-frillion people than a whole mess of COOKIES ? A dozen or so cookies always makes a terrific party gift. We got raves when we added a cookie cutter on a raffia tie around our gift bag. We have a Rainbow Trout cookie that slays.

Alki

Alki has one of the best beaches in the Seattle area. So we made a Sand Lance cookie that you can eat like french fries. They come in 24, 36, or 48 pieces so you can have your own Sand Lances or share with a friend.
